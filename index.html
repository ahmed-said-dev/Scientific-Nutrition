German Seca Examination

This test is conducted using a high-precision German device to determine muscle mass percentage, calorie burning rate, fluid percentage, and to detect whether these fluids are retained and causing weight gain or not. This test provides comprehensive information to help you understand the nature of your body

Free Consultation

Based on the results of the German Seca device examination, the consultant explains all the details to the client and determines a healthy diet plan. Additionally, a program for individual sessions is developed for each client, aiming to ensure the achievement of desired goals through these sessions
